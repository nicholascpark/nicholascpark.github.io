Nicholas Park is a Staff Data Scientist and AI engineer whose career traces a deliberate arc from quantitative foundations to the frontiers of intelligent systems — actuarial mathematics, production ML, reinforcement learning, and now agentic AI. With over nine years building production systems across fintech, insurance, and hardware, and a Master's in Machine Learning from Georgia Tech, he brings both research depth and engineering discipline to problems that demand both. His work centers on the intersection of software engineering, reinforcement learning, and multi-agent AI, with a particular interest in adversarial dynamics — how competing pressures between agents sharpen intelligence. This thread runs through his graduate research on cooperative multi-agent RL and adversarial NLP filtering, and into his current work designing agentic AI solutions at enterprise scale. Through Zealot Analytics, his consulting practice, Park applies this same rigor to organizations navigating the shift toward AI-native systems. For him, rigorous thinking and practical building have never been separate pursuits — they are the same one.
