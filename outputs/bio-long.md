Nicholas Park is a Staff Data Scientist and AI engineer with over nine years of experience building production systems across fintech, insurance, and hardware — a trajectory shaped by the conviction that rigorous thinking and practical building are the same pursuit. He holds an M.Sc. in Computer Science (Machine Learning) from Georgia Tech, where his research focused on cooperative multi-agent reinforcement learning and adversarial methods for improving model robustness. His work sits at the intersection of software engineering, reinforcement learning, and multi-agent AI, with a particular interest in adversarial insight — the idea that competing pressures between agents sharpen intelligence rather than merely test it. Park has led projects spanning agentic AI systems, entity resolution platforms, and predictive modeling, bringing graduate-level research depth to applied problems in insurance, financial services, and beyond. Through Zealot Analytics, his AI consulting venture, he extends this work to organizations navigating the shift from traditional ML pipelines to autonomous, agent-driven architectures.
