Nicholas Park is an AI/ML engineer and researcher whose work centers on building intelligent systems and understanding how they learn — where rigorous quantitative thinking meets hands-on engineering. With over nine years of experience shipping production systems across fintech, insurance, and hardware, and a Master's in Machine Learning from Georgia Tech, he operates at the intersection of software engineering, reinforcement learning, and multi-agent AI. His research in cooperative multi-agent RL and adversarial NLP reflects a sustained interest in how competing pressures between agents sharpen intelligence — a thread that runs from his graduate work through his current focus on agentic AI solutions. Park brings this same dual lens to industry, designing voice agents, entity resolution platforms, and intelligent document processing systems as a Senior AI/ML Engineer at Intact Insurance. Through Zealot Analytics, his AI consulting practice, he applies this research-informed perspective to real-world problems where the gap between prototype and production is the interesting part. For Park, rigorous thinking and practical building have never been separate pursuits — they are the same one.
