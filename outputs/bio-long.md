Nicholas Park is an AI/ML engineer and researcher whose career traces a deliberate arc from quantitative foundations to the frontier of intelligent systems — actuarial mathematics, hardware analytics, machine learning, and now agentic AI. With over nine years building production systems across fintech, insurance, and hardware, and a Master's in Machine Learning from Georgia Tech earned with a 4.0 GPA, he brings both graduate-level research depth and the pragmatism of someone who has shipped real systems under real constraints. His work centers on the intersection of software engineering, reinforcement learning, and multi-agent AI, with a particular interest in adversarial dynamics — how competing pressures between agents sharpen intelligence. This thread runs through his research on cooperative multi-agent RL and adversarial NLI filtering, and into his current work designing agentic AI solutions at Intact Insurance. Through Zealot Analytics, his AI consulting practice, Park bridges research and application, helping organizations build intelligent systems grounded in rigorous thinking. For him, understanding how systems learn and building systems that learn well have never been separate pursuits.
